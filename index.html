About Me

I am a third-year, international student from India, studying Cognitive Science and English at UCLA. I was born in Kolkata, a metropolitan city on the eastern bank of India. Since, I have had the unique pleasure of living in eight cities. The intensive travel goes a long way in explaining my personality. It brought me closer to my family and developed my love for travelling.

Most importantly, however, it acquainted me to the different perspectives that come along with diversity. These experiences are directly related to my presence at UCLA. I picked a university that welcomes diversity and appreciates the unique mindsets of people from different backgrounds.

In terms of my career, I couldn't be further from an answer. My aim is to explore working in different industries for the forseeable future. Some things I plan on devoting time to include Cognitive Science, Financial Consulting, Programming, and Writing.
